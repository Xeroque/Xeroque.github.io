"We need photos that create desire"
In social media, product photography runs the show of online marketing by generating desire trough striking visuals. To perfect product photography you must understand how you want to position your brand and who is your customer, you can learn more about this here. On this occasion I was focused on producing images for used on social media campaigns targeted at craft brewing enthusiasts and was fortunate enough to be able to book an Ab InBev brewery for the shoot.

There are various tricks we used on these photos to enhance desire. All the barrels were carefully sprayed with a water & glycerine mixture so they would appear “sweaty” and fresh out of the freezer, when in fact they were empty and the shoot took hours.
